Maggi


Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of Maggi instant noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • Maggi masala
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the Water: In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add Noodles: Once the water is boiling, break the Maggi noodle cake into the pan and add the Maggi masala seasoning. Stir gently to mix the seasoning.
  3. Cook Noodles: Allow the noodles to cook in the boiling water for about 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cook until the noodles become soft and the water is mostly absorbed.
  4. Add Chopped Vegetables and Maggi Masala
  5. Serve: Your delicious Maggi is now ready to be served. Transfer it to a bowl or a plate and enjoy your quick and satisfying meal!

Enjoy!


Cold Coffe


Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 tablespoons instant coffee powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Chocolate syrup or cocoa powder (optional, for garnish)
  • 1 Scoop Ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add the milk, instant coffee powder, ice cubes and sugar.
  2. Blend the mixture until everything is well combined and the coffee is frothy.
  3. Taste the coffee and adjust the sweetness according to your preference by adding more sugar if needed.
  4. Pour the blended coffee over the ice cubes, filling the glass.
  5. If desired, you can drizzle some chocolate syrup or sprinkle cocoa powder on top for added flavor and presentation.
  6. For a different taste you can add 1 scoop of Ice-cream on top.
  7. Stir the cold coffee gently with a straw or spoon and enjoy your refreshing cold coffee!

Enjoy!
